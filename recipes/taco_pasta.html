Taco Pasta

Very quick and simple taco pasta is a favourite of may of our friends

  • 1 can - lentils
  • 1 large jar - salsa
  • 1 pack - taco seasoning
  • 12 ounces - large pasta shells
  • 1 container - vegtable broth
  • 1 bag - shredded cheese
  • optional - sour cream
  1. add all ingredients except cheese to Instant Pot
  2. use the pressure cook seeting for 6 minutes
  3. quick release the Instant Pot after time completes
  4. Stir in sredded cheese and serve
  5. otional - top with sour cream
